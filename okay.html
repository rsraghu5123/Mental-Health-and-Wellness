Know Your Mental Health

In today's world, where mental health struggles touch the lives of one in every 3rd individuals, it becomes increasingly vital to comprehend our own mental condition. This awareness spans across age groups, highlighting the significance of acknowledging and tending to our mental well-being for a harmonious and enriched existence. Depression is different from regular mood changes and feelings about everyday life. It can affect all aspects of life, including relationships with family, friends and community. It can result from or lead to problems at school and at work.
