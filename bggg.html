Depression

"The only thing more exhausting than being depressed is pretending that you're not."

- Unknown

Depression is a mental health disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest or pleasure in activities, and other symptoms that affect how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Depression in the World

"You are not alone, and you are not to blame. Just as the sun rises every day, so too can you rise from the darkness, shining your light into the world."

- Unknown

Depression affects people of all ages, from all walks of life, and in all countries around the world. It is a leading cause of disability worldwide, and it can have a significant impact on individuals, families, and communities.

Depression in India

"Mental health needs a great deal of attention. It's the final taboo and it needs to be faced and dealt with."

- Adam Ant

India, like many other countries, faces significant challenges related to mental health, including depression. Stigma, lack of awareness, and limited access to mental health services are some of the barriers that prevent people from seeking help.

Know Your Mind Health

In today's world, where mental health struggles touch the lives of one in every 3rd individuals, it becomes increasingly vital to comprehend our own mental condition. This awareness spans across age groups, highlighting the significance of acknowledging and tending to our mental well-being for a harmonious and enriched existence. Depression is different from regular mood changes and feelings about everyday life. It can affect all aspects of life, including relationships with family, friends, and community. It can result from or lead to problems at school and at work.
